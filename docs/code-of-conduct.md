*Anyone* is — by default — able to participate in the **United Paradigms** community.

# Policies
These policies must be followed in all our spaces. Failure to comply may result in being banned in any and all of our communities.

## Harrassment and Discrimination
We *do not* tolerate harassment or discrimination of ourselves or other community members whatsoever. Leave all biases behind when interacting with us or our members.

## Explicit Content
Sexual content and innuendos and gore are *never* allowed anywhere near our community.

## Politics
Our spaces are not the place to consider or debate politics. There are endless places online for political discourse. Please take such elsewhere.

## Be Cool
Name-calling, insults, trolling, spamming, etc., are not allowed. *Just be cool.*
